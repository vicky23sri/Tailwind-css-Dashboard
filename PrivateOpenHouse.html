Private Open House

Mission and Origin

Private Open House was established in Silicon Valley by leading Realtors to enhance the buying and selling process. The initiative aims to ensure optimal representation for both buyers and sellers.

Commitment and Benefits

The service is free of charge and carries no obligation to engage in buying or selling. The goal is to equip agents with advanced tools and technology, provide buyers with comprehensive property listings, and offer sellers accurate property valuation data and confidence in their home sale.

Agent Tools and Buyer Inventory

Our AI driven algorithm will find homes that meet your criteria and you won't find anywhere else and alert you before they hit the market.

Seller Confidence and Privacy

Sellers are provided with data to understand the true value of their properties and tools to ensure a secure and confident sale process. The service places a high priority on user privacy, ensuring all personal information is kept confidential.
